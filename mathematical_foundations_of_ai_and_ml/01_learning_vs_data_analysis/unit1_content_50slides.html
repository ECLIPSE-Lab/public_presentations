    • Prior, likelihood, posterior (qualitative).
  9. Frequentist vs Bayesian workflow (practical)
    • What changes in engineering decisions.
  10. Decision layer separate from inference
    • Predictive probability vs action rule.
  11. No-free-lunch intuition
    • Inductive bias always present.
  12. Curse of dimensionality (conceptual)
    • Why data hunger explodes with dimension.
  13. Recap slide: 6 equations/ideas to remember
